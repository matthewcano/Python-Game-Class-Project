About Attack Heal Counter

Attack Heal Counter is a rock-paper-scissors combat derivative with a few artistic licenses.

Each player starts with 100 health. Both players have the following options: Attack, Heal, Counter, Magic.

Each attack (or defense) does a variable amount of damage or healing depending on the other player's choice.
